about dhanraj shree realtech

Dhanraj Shree Realtech presents premium farmland plots in Madaur, Mathura.

designed for those seeking a harmonious blend of modern connectivity and serene, eco-friendly living. Strategically located at Yamuna Expressway, the project offers excellent accessibility to Delhi, Greater Noida, Agar and major commercial hubs, making it an attractive choice for both lifestyle investment and long-term value appreciation. These well-planned farmhouse plots provide a peaceful retreat away from the hustle of city life while ensuring that essential conveniences remain within easy reach. Surrounded by lush greenery, the development promotes sustainable living with expansive open spaces, fresh air, and a tranquil environment that supports wellness and relaxation. The project is ideal for weekend getaways, private farmhouse residences, or as a high-potential investment for future growth.
